Rangoli

  1. What is Rangoli?

    2. Rangoli is also called as Kolam. It is a traditional art from India. It can be wall art as well

  2. Rangoli’s are decorative design?

    3. Rangoli’s are attractive are decorative design made with help of rice flour on the living room or in front of house every day Morning. During the festival time the Rangoli’s made with bright colors.

  3. How many types of rangoli are there?
    • Form:

      • Circles, cones and lines are form-dominant type of rangoli in India.

    • Ornamental:

      • Ornamental rangoli consists of images of birds, animals, trees, petals, flower and deities

  4. What is the purpose of Rangoli?

    5. The purpose of Rangoli is to give a warm welcome to the guest and also it thought of bring good luck. As well as it’s food for Ants and Birds. It was just mostly done by girls. The Rangoli’s are predominantly made by girls or women.

  5. Significance of kolam:
    • Rice flour is a major ingredient to make kolam or kolam powder .
    • Drawing kolam will help to relieve from stress , enable us to be happy.
    • Drawing kolam enhance people lifestyle.
    • It done by the women folk of the house early in the morning every day.
  6. Decide where to draw your Rangoli?

    7. You can draw it on a flat, dry floor inside or outside of your home, or you can draw it on paper to display anywhere. The earliest Rangoli were created in small 2x2 patterns, but can now be any size, even covering entire floors
